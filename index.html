About Me

As a kid I would often find myself un-screwing electronics. I would intentionally break fans, vacuums and light switches, only to repair them again. I was extremely curious about putting things together and taking them apart.

I took my first programming lessons when I was 8 years old. We used a software called MicroMundos to learn about animations back in Costa Rica where I pretty much spent the first 21 years of my life. I was completely fascinated about the fact that I could tell little turtles what to do by simply typing things into a computer. Not only they would animate and become alive, but they would interact with other objects as well.
